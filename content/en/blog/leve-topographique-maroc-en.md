## What is a Topographic Survey?

A topographic survey is a precise measurement of the land. It captures terrain relief, contour lines, property boundaries, existing buildings, vegetation and underground utilities.

## Why Do You Need One?

Before any construction project, land division or property registration with the ANCFCC, a topographic survey is **mandatory**. Without it, no building permit can be issued.

## The Steps of a Topographic Survey

1. **Site reconnaissance** — initial visit to assess access and complexity
2. **Data collection** — field measurements using GNSS GPS and total stations
3. **Computer processing** — post-processing data with CAD/GIS software
4. **Plan production** — final output validated by the licensed surveying engineer
